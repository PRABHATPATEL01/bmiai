Medical Disclaimer

BMI AI is an informational wellness application and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. All BMI calculations are based on internationally recognized public health standards and clinical growth references.

1. How BMI Is Calculated

Metric Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

Example:

  • Weight = 70 kg
  • Height = 1.75 m
  • BMI = 22.86

Imperial Formula

BMI = (Weight in Pounds × 703) ÷ Height² (inches²)

2. Adult BMI Categories

BMI Range Classification
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Healthy Weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obesity

3. Pediatric & Teen BMI Standards

For users under 20 years old, BMI AI uses age-based and sex-based percentile interpretation according to CDC and WHO growth standards.

Percentile Classification
Below 5th percentile Underweight
5th – 85th percentile Healthy Weight
85th – 95th percentile Overweight
Above 95th percentile Obesity

5. Clinical Limitations of BMI

BMI is a screening tool and not a direct measure of body fat.

  • Athletes
  • Bodybuilders
  • Pregnant individuals
  • Elderly adults
  • High muscle mass individuals
  • Certain ethnic populations
